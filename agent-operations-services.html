Common questions

What tools do you use?

We're tool-agnostic. Depending on your stack, we might use Grafana, LangSmith, custom scripts, or a combination. We recommend what fits your existing infrastructure — no vendor lock-in.

Do we need to change our agent frameworks?

No. We work with whatever you're running — LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGPT, custom Python, or any combination. The monitoring layer sits alongside your existing code.

What if we want ongoing support after the engagement?

We offer optional monthly retainers for ongoing monitoring, optimization reviews, and incident support. Or you can transition to our platform product when it launches.

How quickly can you start?

Most engagements kick off within 1-2 weeks of signing. For urgent compliance situations, we can often accelerate.

What do we own at the end?

Everything. All monitoring setups, documentation, runbooks, and training materials are yours. No proprietary dependencies or ongoing licensing required.
