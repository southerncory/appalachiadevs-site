Common questions

What tools do you use?

We're tool-agnostic. Depending on your stack, we might use Grafana, custom dashboards, or integrate with tools you already have. No vendor lock-in.

Do we need to change our agent frameworks?

No. We work with whatever you're running — LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGPT, custom Python, anything. The monitoring layer sits alongside your existing code.

What if we want ongoing support?

We offer ongoing partnerships for continuous monitoring, policy updates, and on-call support. Ask us about retainer arrangements.

How quickly can you start?

Most engagements kick off within 1-2 weeks of signing. For urgent situations, we can often accelerate.

What do we own at the end?

Everything. All tooling, configurations, documentation, and training materials are yours. No proprietary dependencies.
