Questions

What do I need to get started?

Your AA employee ID and password. For managed plans, that's it—we handle the rest. For self-hosted, you'll need a server and some technical experience.

Is my login secure?

Your credentials are encrypted and stored only on your dedicated agent instance. We never access your account directly. The agent acts on your behalf using browser automation.

What if AA changes their portals?

Managed subscribers get automatic updates. We monitor for portal changes and push fixes. Self-hosted users get notified and can pull updates from the repo.

Why residential IP?

AA portals flag datacenter IPs. Managed plans route through residential connections so your agent doesn't get blocked or trigger extra verification.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime via email.
