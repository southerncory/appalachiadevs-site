The orchestration layer

We don't replace your wallets — we unify them. One dashboard, one policy engine, one audit trail across every agent economic system.

Universal Agent Registry

Every agent, every identity (ERC-8004, DID, custom), every wallet connection — inventoried and tracked in one place.

Cross-Rail Policy Engine

Define spending rules that work across Coinbase, Stripe, AP2, and any other rail. Per-agent limits, approval workflows, category restrictions.

Unified Transaction View

See every agent transaction across every wallet in one dashboard. Filter by agent, by rail, by time, by amount.
