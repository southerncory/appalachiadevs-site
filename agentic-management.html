Three pillars of agent operations

Everything we do maps to these three capabilities.

Visibility

Know what every agent is doing, in real-time.

  • Agent registry and inventory
  • Real-time activity monitoring
  • Performance and health dashboards
  • Anomaly detection and alerts

Governance

Control what agents are allowed to do.

  • Organization-wide policy rules
  • Rate limits and access controls
  • Approval workflows for sensitive actions
  • Cross-framework enforcement

Compliance

Prove what agents did and why.

  • Complete audit trails
  • Decision logging and traceability
  • Export-ready compliance reports
  • SOC2, HIPAA, FINRA alignment
