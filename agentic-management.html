The orchestration layer

A unified control plane that sits above your agent infrastructure. Framework-agnostic, deployment-flexible, built for enterprise scale.

Agent Registry

Every agent registered, versioned, and tracked. Know what's deployed, where it's running, and who owns it. Full lifecycle visibility.

Policy Engine

Define organization-wide rules: rate limits, allowed APIs, data access controls, spending caps. Enforce consistently across all agents.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live dashboards showing agent activity, performance metrics, and health status. Know what every agent is doing, right now.

Automated alerts when behavior deviates from baseline. Catch runaway loops, excessive API calls, and policy violations before damage spreads.

Compliance & Audit

Every action logged. Export-ready reports for SOC2, HIPAA, and internal audits. Prove what your agents did and why.
